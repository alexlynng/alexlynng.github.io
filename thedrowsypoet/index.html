Six of Crows by Leigh Bardugo


"The water hears and understands. The ice does not forgive."

I didn't think anything could top the Shadow and Bone Trilogy, but boy was I wrong. Six of Crows is a sweeping epic tale with six main characters, five different perspectives, complex and detailed world-building that is seamlessly integrated into the story, flashbacks to character's histories, and a perfect blend of darkness, magic, humor, action, and romance.

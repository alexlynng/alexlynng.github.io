Hi, my name is Alex. I am a 21 year old student at the University of New Mexico. I am double majoring in Journalism and Mass Communications and Psychology. I want to pursue a career in advertising because I love the fast-paced environment. I also love to read and write. My top favorite books are:

  • The Strange and Beautiful Sorrows of Ava Lavender by Leslye Walton
  • The Disappearances by Emily Bain Murphy
  • The Night Circus by Erin Morgenstern

The Drowsy Poet came about because I had to come up with a brand for myself in one of my classes. I called it "The Drowsy Poet" because I love to write poetry and I always seem to need coffee to keep me awake. Now, I continue to use this brand name for all of my projects because I think the name describes me perfectly.
